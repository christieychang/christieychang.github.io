Under the research project Modeling and Understanding Human Routine Behavior

My role as a research assistant was to analyze and organize driving data files that would ultimately be used in a car simulation app aiming to improve aggressive driving behavior.

The data files I worked with were collected from sensors places in vehicles. Previously before I joined the team, the researchers conducted driving studies, collecting data regarding drivers' speed as well as throttle and brake pressures.

My main task was to create a Java program that reads through these files of data that was then organized and placed into new files readeable to the car simulation app.
