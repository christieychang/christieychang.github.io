Hi I'm Christie!


​​​​​​I am a senior at Carnegie Mellon University studying Information Systems with minors in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Engineering Studies. My interests lie in user experience and user interface design.

For the first two years in college, I studied Mechanical Engineering before making the switch to Information Systems. While I still love the problem-solving aspect in the engineering field, I discovered how much I enjoyed coding and creating UI designs. I delved myself into the UX field, gaining insight and experience from listening to UX podcasts (I'd recommend User Defenders) and working on projects focusing on the user experience.

With my career, I want to create something that matters.

Outside of my academics, my main involvements are in Greek Life, A Cappella, and First-year Orientation. Through these involvements on campus, I have learned invaluable skills in leadership, conflict management, team management, event planning, and other skills I would not learn in a classroom. In my free time, I love being around nature, painting and sketching.

Ask me questions! Email me at cchang2@andrew.cmu.edu or message me on LinkedIn!
