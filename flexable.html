Flexable provides pop up childcare at professional and personal events, offices, retail stores, and other businesses.

I created high-fidelity mockups for Flexable's software system, designing for three users: the administrators, caregivers, and customer/clients. I worked directly with the developer and CEO of Ahez Co., creating a concept map to visualize the flow of software system for the three users. Below are a few of the mockups. Please contact me if you would like to learn more.
