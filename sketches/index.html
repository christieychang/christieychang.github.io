I grew up taking art classes, learning how to use different mediums (including pencil and pen work, acrylic painting, and water color painting). However, it wasn't until college when I learned how to express my ideas in drawing/sketching form.

When I decided to pursue a career in UX design while at Carnegie Mellon University, I took two drawing courses in the School of Design (Methodology of Visualization with Professor Matt Zywica and Drawing Spaces with Professor Mark Mentzer), learning how to express my ideas effectively through drawing.
